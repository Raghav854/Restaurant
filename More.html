ABOUT US


We, at Ichiraku Ramen try to make some the most delicious ramen. Started in 1983 with a small portable cart to owning and running more than 200 stores all over Japan is quiet a magnificent feat. We only use top quality of ingredients in all of our dishes. Even after 40 Years of running our motto didn’t change, that is
"勤勉さと敬意をもって奉仕する"
which means “Serve With Diligence And Respect”.
Arigatougozaimashita
